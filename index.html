About Us

Nana's Cookies is a family owned bakery dedicated to delicious cookies for you and your own family! All of our treats are made from scratch with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love! Order online or give us a call! We look forward to baking for you! :)

Frequently Asked Questions

When will my order arrive?
You can expect your cookies within 2 to 5 business days of the order being processed. We do our best to get your order out as fast as possible!
How many cookies can I order?
As many as you like! Keep in mind that the more cookies you order, the longer it will take Nana to make and bake them for you.
Is Nana the only one making cookies?
Nana makes all of the cookie recipes herself, that's where the cookie magic comes from! She does have a couple of great helpers when baking though. One Nana can only do so much at once!
